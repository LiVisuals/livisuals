About LiVisuals

You live the moment, we make it last.

Hi, my name is Livingston, and I’m the founder of LiVisuals.

Coming from a family of photographers, I’ve always been surrounded by cameras and creativity. But it wasn’t until I discovered videography that I truly found my passion. There was something about capturing real moments in motion—the emotions, the energy, the story—that drew me in.

I started out filming uni events and birthday parties, which helped me find my feet in the industry. But everything shifted when I had the opportunity to film my first weddings. That’s when I knew I wanted to tell love stories in my own way—blending cinematic beauty with a raw, documentary-style approach that captures the day as it truly felt.

LiVisuals is more than just a photo and video business — we’re a team dedicated to capturing intimate and personal moments, creating films and images that reflect the genuine emotions and unique stories of each couple. Every couple has their own rhythm, their own quiet moments, laughter, and energy, and our goal is to preserve that essence in a way that feels timeless and true to you. Whether it’s the joy in your first look, the stillness before you walk down the aisle, or the energy of the dance floor, we’re there to catch the moments you’ll want to relive again and again.

At the heart of it all, I’m just a Tamil boy with a camera, passionate about storytelling and inspired by the people I meet. My goal is simple: to create timeless wedding films that feel personal, emotional, and real.

Thank you for being here and for being part of my journey. I hope I’m part of yours.

— Livingston | LiVisuals
